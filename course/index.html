What you'll gain

Three pillars of growth

The Entrepreneur Bootcamp combines inspirational reflection, structured goal-setting, and ongoing peer accountability into a proven weekly rhythm.

🎥

Inspiration

Documentaries of real entrepreneurs overcoming obstacles in contexts just like yours. See what's possible and reflect on your own story.

🎯

Planning & Goals

Translate big dreams into tangible weekly sales targets. Calculate the steps, identify obstacles, and build a roadmap you can actually follow.

🤝

Peer Support

A peer circle to share talents, celebrate wins, and offer encouragement. Accountability from women who understand your exact challenges.

Who it's for

Built for women building businesses
