Who We Are

SENLA Research Hub is a non-profit research organization registered in Uganda, dedicated to evidence-based socio-economic policy research and capacity building.

Mission

Develop rapid and timely solutions through evidence-based research to address long- and short-term socioeconomic challenges facing Africa.

Vision

Serve as a hub of transformational, policy-oriented socioeconomic research for Africa.

Legal Status

Company limited by guarantee (non-profit); income and property used exclusively to advance objectives.
