Who We Are

SENLA Research Hub is an Africa-based and world-class research, training, and consultancy network dedicated to offering alternative pathways to economic development with a focus on Africa and other emerging markets. The network provides independent and comprehensive analyses of political, social, and economic conditions in Africa and beyond, with the view to proffering timely and workable solutions that transcend initial conditions.

Why We Exist

There has been a need for cross collaboration between African nations, especially in the social research realm. As the continent continues to develop, we require accurate analysis and solutions to the problems at hand, aiming to enable efficient growth within Africa.

What Makes Us Unique

We have a diversified team of qualified scholars with years of experience in academic research on socio-economic matters of Africa.
