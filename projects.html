Financial Literacy and Aspirations

Lead Researchers: Dr. Ester Agasha

This project, conducted in Eastern Uganda, focused on improving women's financial literacy and aspirations through mobile-based games and educational interventions. Results showed significant improvements in financial literacy and empowerment.

Green Riches, Golden Assets

Lead Researchers: Ester Agasha, Nathalie Nyanga

Using geospatial tools, this project examined the relationship between environmental greenery, asset ownership, and dietary patterns in Uganda. Findings highlighted the influence of socio-economic status on nutrition.

Entrepreneurship Education in Uganda

Lead Researcher: Dr. Sarah Nabachwa

A collaboration between academia and local businesses to develop tailored educational content for entrepreneurs. The project has supported hundreds of Ugandan entrepreneurs through workshops and mentorship.
